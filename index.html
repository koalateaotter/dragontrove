DragonTrove™

Budget Like A Dragon

Your treasure deserves a guardian.

DragonTrove is a budgeting app to protect your hoard of envelopes. Because why can't budgeting have an element of fun? Create gold piles for categories you need to track — groceries, gas, dining out. Intentionally track your spending as your dragon breathes fire, burning through your trove.

This app was built for my family to track our trove and reduce the resistance of other budgeting apps. I use this app with my family every day. You should too!

Quick access, offline first, and privacy first. All your data stays on your phone or with the people you share a budget with. Share budgets with others through iCloud to budget together. Transactions can be added with balances updated in real time.

Features

Envelope Budgeting

Divide your income into gold piles. Track every transaction against your budget in real time.

iCloud Sync

Syncs privately across all your Apple devices. Share budgets with family or a partner in real time.

Offline First

Works completely offline. No account required, no subscription, no login.

Privacy First

All your data stays on your device. We collect nothing. Your financial data belongs to you.

Voice Entry

Add transactions by voice. Just say "$75 at Costco for groceries" and DragonTrove fills in the details.

Spending Trends

Track spending over months. See where your gold goes with beautiful charts and fund goal tracking.

Themes

Choose Dragon Hoard or Classic theme. Pick your dragon's color. Make it yours.

Your Data

Import and export your data via CSV anytime. No data lock-in, ever.
